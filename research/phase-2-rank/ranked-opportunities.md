## Scoring Methodology

Each posting scored 0-100 across 5 dimensions:
1. **Skills Match (30%)** — % of role's required skills Diego has
2. **Role Level Fit (20%)** — Seniority appropriateness (mid/senior = best)
3. **AI/Automation Relevance (20%)** — LLMs, agents, automation, AI ops
4. **Salary Potential (15%)** — Listed salary or market estimate
5. **Growth/Interest (15%)** — Leverages ops+AI+engineering combo; mission interest

**Remote filter:** LinkedIn postings with `Is Remote: False` are D-tier by default (Diego can only do remote work). Several LinkedIn postings showed `Is Remote: False` despite "Remote" in location — treated as non-remote due to the field value.
