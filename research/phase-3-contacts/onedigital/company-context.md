# Company Context: OneDigital

## Overview
OneDigital is a national insurance, financial services, and HR consulting firm serving employers and individuals, headquartered in Atlanta, GA. With 5,000–10,000 employees, they have built a proprietary AI product layer (Impact Studio) and deployed a fleet of "AI coworkers" — specialized agents trained on benefits, compliance, and client data — to augment their 3,000+ consultant workforce. They describe themselves as "fierce advocates of health, success, and financial security."

## Recent News
- Dec 2025: Completed $2.3B strategic investment from Stone Point Capital and CPP Investments, valuing OneDigital above $7B — signals major growth phase and tech investment capacity
- Early 2026: Acquired two RIA firms ($1.4B Silicon Valley Retirement Services + $330M Derse Morgan) as part of wealth management expansion
- Nov 2025: Vinay Gidwaney featured in Plan Adviser for training AI coworkers; Ben (AI benefits agent) had processed 115,000+ client assignments
- Sep 2025: Microsoft published case study on OneDigital's Azure-powered AI coworker platform, saving 1,000+ person-hours annually

## Culture Signals
- Strong "human-centered AI" philosophy — AI augments people, does not replace them
- Framing AI agents as coworkers with portraits, managers, and onboarding mirrors how Diego thinks about AI ops
- CPO Vinay Gidwaney is a serial entrepreneur (Maxwell Health, Control-F1); the product org skews startup-minded inside a larger firm
- Named one of the 40 fastest-growing companies; HR team recognized as Top 10 HR Team globally
- Active LinkedIn culture; leadership posts frequently about AI, innovation, and industry transformation

## Tech Stack
