# Company Context: Rippling

## Overview

Rippling is a workforce management platform that unifies HR, IT, and Finance into a single system of record. Founded by Parker Conrad (also co-founder of Zenefits), Rippling automates payroll, benefits, device management, spend, and compliance across 25+ integrated products. Their core pitch is "compound software" — each product is more powerful because it runs on a shared data model. They serve businesses from startups to enterprise, with a particularly strong position in the SMB/mid-market HR-tech space.

## Recent News

- March 2026: Rippling launched Rippling AI — an AI layer across the entire platform helping managers make decisions in HR, IT, and Finance. Major product announcement with high internal engagement (3,500+ LinkedIn reactions on announcement post).
- December 2025: Multiple platform releases including sandbox testing environments, bulk approvals, enhanced HCM/Finance/IT features, and contractor payment improvements.
- May 2025: Raised $450M Series G at $16.8B valuation. Investors include Baillie Gifford, Elad Gil, Goldman Sachs, GIC, Sands Capital, and Y Combinator. No IPO plans near-term due to CEO preference for growth over profitability framing.
- 2025: Named to CNBC Disruptor 50 list. Revenue growing 30%+ annually, crossing $570M ARR.
- October 2025: Employee Scheduling product became company's fastest to reach $1M in sales.
- 2025: Launched Talent Signal — AI performance evaluation tool using task-specific signals (e.g., call transcript analysis for support reps).
- 2025: Launched Workflow Studio for no-code cross-business automation with real-time triggers and conditional logic.
