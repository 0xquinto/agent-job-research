# Company Context: NXLog

## Overview

NXLog is a bootstrapped log management and telemetry pipeline company founded in 2009 by Botond Botyanszki. The company builds agents and a centralized platform (NXLog Platform) that collect, process, and route log/telemetry data from Windows, Linux, macOS, OT, and cloud environments to SIEMs and observability tools. ~90+ employees, fully remote across 30+ countries, $15M annual revenue as of 2025. No outside funding — self-sustaining and profitable.

## Recent News

- Mar 26, 2026: Posted the Agent Lead Developer (Rust/C & AI Orchestration) role — the most technically advanced hire they have made publicly, signaling a strategic move into AI-powered agent behavior.
- Jan 2026: NXLog Platform 1.11 released — adds data volume monitoring, 2FA enforcement, OpenShift Helm support, and improved TLS diagnostics.
- Dec 2024 / Jan 2025: Leadership transition — Harald Reisinger appointed CEO; Botond Botyanszki transitions to CTO role to focus on technology roadmap. Announced at same time as push into observability and telemetry pipeline management market.
- 2025: ~50 new partnerships and 54 new enterprise clients including Jaguar Land Rover, eBay, Coca-Cola Bottling, BBVA Mexico, and SAS Institute.
- Feb 2026: Botond published "Why Telemetry Becomes Critical Infrastructure in 2026" — frames AI agents making autonomous infrastructure decisions as the reason observability must become a first-class accountability layer.

## Culture Signals

- Remote-first since inception (team across 30+ countries, 5 continents).
- Glassdoor rating: 4.0 overall but 3.4 culture/values — some reports of micromanagement and passive-aggressive communication style in management. Interview experience positive at 67%.
- Laszlo Budai's recommendation for a previous recruiter mentioned difficulty hiring "good C developers" — suggests they value deep systems programming skill and will push candidates hard.
- Company emphasizes practical impact over buzzword adoption: product releases focus on reliability, compliance, and operational controls rather than AI/ML hype.
- The Agent Lead Developer role is an anomaly — explicitly hybrid Rust/C + AI orchestration — suggesting this is Botond's personal technical initiative.

## Tech Stack

- Core agent: C (NXLog Agent 6.x) — heavily cross-platform (Windows, Linux, macOS, AIX, Solaris)
- Platform backend: Rust (actively hiring Senior Rust developers since 2025)
- Frontend: React, JavaScript
- Infrastructure: Kubernetes / Helm, OpenShift, OpenSSL 3.5 LTS
- Protocols: TLS, HTTP/S, OpenTelemetry (OTLP)
- Integrations: Osquery, Okta, Azure SSO, GitLab SSO, Google SSO
- Data destinations: Splunk, Elasticsearch, SIEM platforms broadly
- AI orchestration: Not yet in production — this role is the build
