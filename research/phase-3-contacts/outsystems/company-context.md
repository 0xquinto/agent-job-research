# Company Context: OutSystems

## Overview

OutSystems is a low-code application development platform vendor headquartered in Boston, MA (founded in Lisbon, Portugal). They serve enterprise customers across 80 countries and 21 industries, and have sharply repositioned around Agentic AI in 2025-2026. Their platform — especially Agent Workbench — enables enterprises to build, deploy, and govern AI agents at scale with built-in compliance and security. Expert Services is the team that delivers this work directly inside customer environments.

## Culture Signals

- Technical depth valued: Expert Services team is described as the bridge between R&D/Labs and customer delivery. Director Gonçalo Santos is actively pursuing ML certifications (Stanford, 2024-2025), which signals a culture where even managers are expected to stay technically sharp.
- "People obsessed with outcomes": Job posting language emphasizes customer obsession and accomplishment through successful delivery — not a body-shop consultancy mindset.
- Long employee tenure: Director Santos has been at OutSystems 9+ years, climbing internally. HR generalist Katie Shaw at 4 years. Loren Mastronardi (People Dev Lead) joined from Dropbox in Jan 2026. Signals retention culture.
- Global but remote-friendly: US-based roles are fully remote. Core engineering/leadership is Portugal-based. Time zone overlap is a real operational consideration for Expert Services managers.
- Growth and performance framing: 2026 performance strategy underway (Loren Mastronardi's mandate). This is a company in deliberate scale mode, not a coast.
