# Company Context: Grafana Labs

## Overview
Grafana Labs builds the open-source observability platform used by 20M+ users worldwide, anchored by Grafana, Loki, Tempo, and Mimir. The company provides a unified "single pane of glass" for metrics, logs, traces, and now AI-powered analytics. In 2025-2026 they are aggressively pushing into AI-assisted observability with Grafana Assistant (natural language queries over telemetry data) and the Grafana Cloud Knowledge Graph (full-stack topology for incident correlation).
