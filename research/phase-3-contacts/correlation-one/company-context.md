# Company Context: Correlation One

## Overview

Correlation One is an AI enablement and workforce development company that trains enterprise, government, and global institutional clients to build human capabilities for the AI economy. Founded in 2015 and headquartered in New York City, the company operates across 50+ countries and has trained 500,000+ professionals through cohort-based programs in generative AI, data analytics, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. Key clients include Fortune 500 enterprises, the U.S. Department of Defense, U.S. Department of State, USAID, and Amazon (via Career Choice).
