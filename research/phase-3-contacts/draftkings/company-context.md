# Company Context: DraftKings

## Overview
DraftKings (NASDAQ: DKNG) is a Boston-based digital sports entertainment and gaming company offering sportsbook, daily fantasy sports, iGaming casino, and — as of late 2025 — CFTC-regulated prediction markets. The company went public via SPAC in 2020 and has grown to approximately 6,400 employees across 11 global offices. They are known for an aggressive expansion strategy in newly regulated U.S. sports betting markets and are now entering prediction/event contracts as a major new vertical.
