# Company Context: Rocket Money

## Overview
Rocket Money (formerly Truebill, rebranded 2022) is a personal finance app owned by Rocket Companies, serving 5+ million members. Core products include subscription management, bill negotiation, budgeting, and deposit/savings features. The company is actively expanding its banking product suite, including a forthcoming high-yield deposit account, positioning itself as a full-service financial management platform rather than just a budgeting tool.

## Recent News (last 3 months)
- **Feb 2026:** Rocket Companies (parent) announced a 3-year strategic alliance with Compass International Holdings to integrate Rocket Mortgage as Compass's preferred digital mortgage partner — signals broad Rocket Companies expansion and cross-sell momentum that may accelerate Rocket Money banking ambitions
- **Dec 2025:** Rocket Money CEO Haroon Mokhtarzada joined Range's Board of Advisors as part of Range's $60M Series C — signals Haroon's personal brand investment in broader fintech ecosystem
- **Ongoing:** Rocket Money is actively hiring across G&A, product, and operations — the Business Operations Manager deposit role is live and the G&A recruiter (Gregorio Labrado) has been actively posting roles in the last 2-5 months
- **Active product:** Kirsten Klotz (VP/GM) expanded scope from lending to deposits + credit cards — indicating deposit products is a current growth priority, not a legacy function

## Culture Signals
- Mission-driven language: "meaningfully improve the financial prosperity of millions"
- Emphasizes ownership mentality — job posting language includes "thrive in ambiguity" and "act as an owner"
- Collaborative small-team structure: only 221 employees, multiple directors report directly to CEO
- Hack weeks and cross-functional collaboration noted in engineering culture
- Hires from diverse industries, not just fintech
- Commitment to team growth and personal development

## Tech Stack
- Engineering: TypeScript, Node.js, GraphQL, Postgres

- Banking partner: NBKC Bank (FDIC-insured partner for deposit products)
