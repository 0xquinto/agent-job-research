# Company Context: CACI International

## Overview

CACI International is a Fortune 500 defense and national security IT services company (~26,000 employees) headquartered in Reston, VA. They provide technology and services to US government agencies across intelligence, DoD, DHS, and federal civilian markets. Their strategic shift under CEO John Mengucci has been from a services-centric model to a technology and solutions provider, with AI and software-defined capabilities as the centerpiece of that transformation.

## Culture Signals

- Nearly 40% veterans; strong mission-first ethos ("expanding the limits of national security")
- CEO frames AI as "enabling tech" — amplifying developers, not replacing them
- CTO Bales described software as CACI's "superpower"; aggressive push toward software-defined solutions
- Active in classified/IC work — most AI roles require security clearances, some polygraph
- Internal AI Acceleration Center (A2C) run by Patrick Medina specifically supports programs adopting AI — signals a company investing in internal AI enablement, not just customer-facing AI
- Glassdoor Best Place to Work 2026; cited for collaborative environment, career growth, leadership, work-life balance
- "Cup of CACI" hiring events suggest active, relationship-based recruiting culture
