## Top Tier — Best for Diego's Profile

These platforms have the highest density of roles matching smart contract security, DeFi protocol work, and Web3 ops/AI.

### 1. cryptocurrencyjobs.co
- **URL:** https://cryptocurrencyjobs.co
- **Specialization:** Curated DeFi, infrastructure, and protocol jobs. Manually vetted — not a firehose. Strong showing for auditors, smart contract engineers, security leads.
- **Activity:** Actively maintained as of March 2026. Weekly newsletter published 2026-03-26 featuring roles at Wintermute, Paradex, a16z Crypto, Lido, Aave, Uniswap, Ethena Labs.
- **Quality:** High. Human-curated. Low spam. Companies like Wintermute, Morpho, Uniswap, CoW DAO, Lido, OpenZeppelin post here.
- **Remote-friendly:** Yes — majority of listings are remote.
- **Diego fit:** HIGH. Has dedicated DeFi section with auditor roles (e.g., Nethermind Smart Contract Auditor, OpenZeppelin security roles visible). Actively posts roles that match Solidity/security/DeFi ops intersection.
- **Notes:** Subscribe to the weekly newsletter (Substack). This is the single highest-signal board for Diego's exact profile.

### 2. web3.career
- **URL:** https://web3.career
- **Specialization:** Broad aggregator — engineering, dev, security, smart contracts. Has dedicated filters for `security+smart-contract`, `solidity`, `defi+security`.
- **Activity:** Active March 2026. Posts Solidity jobs (4+ new in March 2026), DeFi security jobs. Publishes a "Web3 Career Intelligence Report."
- **Quality:** High volume, decent quality. Some older postings visible but easy to filter by recency.
- **Remote-friendly:** Yes — remote filter available.
- **Diego fit:** HIGH. Specific filter combinations like `web3.career/security+smart-contract-jobs` and `web3.career/solidity-jobs` surface exactly the right roles. Veridise, Tenderly, EigenLabs, OpenZeppelin appear here.
- **Notes:** Best used with specific tag-combo URLs rather than browsing the homepage. Bookmark: `web3.career/security+smart-contract-jobs`, `web3.career/defi+security-jobs`, `web3.career/solidity-jobs`.

### 3. cryptojobslist.com
- **URL:** https://cryptojobslist.com
- **Specialization:** Broad crypto/blockchain. Has dedicated smart contract, remote, and developer sections. Good editorial context on each category.
- **Activity:** Active March 2026 — "128 new in Mar 2026" shown on homepage, updated hourly.
- **Quality:** High. Includes salary ranges. Mix of startups and established companies (Chainlink, Eigen Labs, Polygon, Stellar Development Foundation, Alchemy, Binance, Galaxy, JPMorgan Chase blockchain roles).
- **Remote-friendly:** Yes — explicit remote filter.
- **Diego fit:** HIGH. Smart contract auditor roles visible (SigIntZero Senior Smart Contract Auditor, SigIntZero Smart Contract Auditor Rust — both match Diego's Rust + auditing background). The site's editorial framing on smart contract roles explicitly calls out Foundry, Slither, fuzz testing, and auditing pedigree as top signals — exactly Diego's stack.
- **Notes:** Use `cryptojobslist.com/smart-contract` and `cryptojobslist.com/remote` filtered searches. Has blog post on top smart contract audit companies (Feb 2026) that's useful for identifying which firms are actively hiring.

### 4. jobs.nascent.xyz (Nascent VC Job Board)
- **URL:** https://jobs.nascent.xyz
- **Specialization:** Jobs at Nascent's DeFi portfolio companies: 0x, Aztec Protocol, Balancer, Code4rena, Alethea AI, Cantrip/Ponder, etc.
- **Activity:** Active. Code4rena listed as a portfolio company with open roles.
- **Quality:** Very high signal — all companies are vetted by a DeFi-native VC. Small, curated set (~50 companies).
- **Remote-friendly:** Yes — most portfolio companies are remote-first.
- **Diego fit:** HIGH. Code4rena is literally on this board. Companies like 0x (ERC-721, DeFi infra), Aztec (privacy L2), Balancer (DeFi liquidity) all match Diego's DeFi/security background. AI + blockchain intersection via Alethea AI.
- **Notes:** Less volume, higher signal. Check quarterly. Also useful for identifying which companies in the Nascent portfolio might want Diego's profile directly.

### 5. jobs.protocol.ai (Protocol Labs Network Board)
- **URL:** https://jobs.protocol.ai
- **Specialization:** Protocol Labs network — decentralized infrastructure, storage, compute, and security research. StarkWare, and other ZK/infra companies post here.
- **Activity:** Active March 2026 — found StarkWare Blockchain Security Research Team Lead posting here.
- **Quality:** High — Protocol Labs-vetted ecosystem companies.
- **Remote-friendly:** Yes — the network is globally distributed by design.
- **Diego fit:** HIGH for security research, ZK, and infrastructure. StarkWare posting for "Blockchain Security Research Team Lead" is a direct match for Diego's auditing + research profile.
- **Notes:** Smaller board but very high quality. Good for security research and infra-layer roles.

### 6. a16zcrypto.com/jobs (a16z Crypto Portfolio Board)
- **URL:** https://a16zcrypto.com/jobs (may also be at jobs.a16z.com — verify URL)
- **Specialization:** Roles across a16z's crypto portfolio — some of the best-funded Web3 companies.
- **Activity:** Active. a16z Crypto appears in cryptocurrency jobs newsletters (March 2026).
- **Quality:** Very high — every company has VC backing from one of the top crypto funds.
- **Remote-friendly:** Varies by company, but many are remote-friendly.
- **Diego fit:** HIGH. Senior/experienced roles, DeFi and infra focus. Companies like Paradigm, a16z portfolio DAOs and protocols are a match for Diego's operations + security profile.
- **Notes:** The direct URL `jobs.a16z.com/crypto` returned a 404 in testing — use `a16zcrypto.com/jobs` as the working path, or check listings via cryptocurrencyjobs.co which surfaces a16z portfolio openings.

## Worth Checking — Decent But Narrower Fit

### 7. crypto.jobs
- **URL:** https://crypto.jobs
- **Specialization:** One of the oldest crypto job boards (since 2017). Focuses on established companies. Has 1,363 current positions across 802 companies (as of Feb 2026).
- **Activity:** Active. Published Web3 Hiring Trends 2026 report (Feb 2026) and full career guide. 87% of listings offer remote.
- **Quality:** Good but broader than Diego needs. Includes non-technical roles. Top employers include Dash Core Group, Coinbase.
- **Remote-friendly:** Yes — 87% of listings remote.
- **Diego fit:** MEDIUM. Good coverage but less specialized for security/DeFi than the top-tier boards. Worth a monthly check, not a daily one.

### 8. remote3.co
- **URL:** https://remote3.co
- **Specialization:** Remote-first web3 jobs. 22,000+ total listings (includes historical). Strong Telegram community (3,800+ members).
- **Activity:** Last "new jobs" count cited Sep 2025, though site appears active.
- **Quality:** Mixed — high volume means more noise. Better for browsing volume than curating signal.
- **Remote-friendly:** Yes — exclusively remote.
- **Diego fit:** MEDIUM. Has specific filters for Solidity, Rust, Defi, InfoSec, Management/Finance. Useful for casting a wider net.
- **Notes:** Use specific filters: remote3.co filters for `Solidity`, `Rust`, `InfoSec`, `DeFi`.

### 9. sailonchain.com
- **URL:** https://sailonchain.com
- **Specialization:** Aggregates 1,744+ jobs from 1,240+ blockchain companies. Engineering, marketing, product.
- **Activity:** Site active, listing count current.
- **Quality:** Unknown curation quality. High volume aggregator.
- **Remote-friendly:** Yes.
- **Diego fit:** MEDIUM. Good for volume discovery, not quality signal.

### 10. thecryptorecruiters.io

## Competitive Audit Platforms (Not Job Boards — But Adjacent)

These are not job boards but are directly relevant to Diego's auditing skills. Participation builds reputation that leads to full-time roles.

### Code4rena
- **URL:** https://code4rena.com
- **What it is:** Competitive audit contest platform. 16,600+ registered wardens. Protocols pay for audit contests; researchers compete for payouts.
- **Full-time angle:** Code4rena also has direct full-time job listings (visible on Nascent board). Strong audit contest performance here is a hiring signal for firms like OpenZeppelin, Spearbit, and others.
- **Diego fit:** HIGH — Foundry, Slither, and the Aderyn/Halmos/Medusa stack Diego uses is exactly what top performers here use.

### Sherlock (sherlock.xyz)
- **URL:** https://sherlock.xyz
- **What it is:** Combines senior-led audits ("Blackthorn") with contest-scale coverage. Bug bounty programs with high payouts.
- **Full-time angle:** Sherlock's audit competition track record translates to job offers from protocols.
- **Diego fit:** HIGH — strong DeFi security focus aligns directly with Diego's DeFi protocol analysis skills.

### Immunefi (immunefi.com)
- **URL:** https://immunefi.com
- **What it is:** Largest web3 bug bounty platform. Also hires internally (Smart Contract Triager role seen Aug 2025, DeFi Security Analyst role seen).
- **Full-time angle:** Internal roles at Immunefi (triager, security analyst) are a direct match for Diego. Also, bounty track record signals expertise to employers.
- **Diego fit:** HIGH — both for internal roles and for building public audit reputation.
